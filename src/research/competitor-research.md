# Market Research - Local Digital Marketing Agencies (NL)

## Competitor Analysis

### Major Players in Dutch Local Marketing Space

1. **Lokaal7** - https://lokaal7.nl
   - Focus: Local SEO for small businesses
   - Pricing: ~€99-299/mo
   - Strengths: Dutch market specialist, transparent reporting
   - Weaknesses: Limited ad management, basic tools

2. **Online Marketing Partners** - https://onlinemarketingpartners.nl
   - Focus: Full-service digital marketing
   - Pricing: €500-2000/mo
   - Strengths: Comprehensive, Google Partner certified
   - Weaknesses: Expensive for small businesses, slow onboarding

3. **GrowthRepublic** - https://growthrepublic.nl
   - Focus: Growth hacking, startups
   - Pricing: Custom
   - Strengths: Data-driven, modern approach
   - Weaknesses: Too expensive for local SMBs

4. **Byte** - https://byte.nl
   - Focus: Web design + marketing
   - Pricing: €200-1000/mo
   - Strengths: Design quality, reliability
   - Weaknesses: Traditional, slower to adapt

5. **Meteor** - https://meteor.se
   - Focus: SaaS marketing tools
   - Pricing: Subscription based
   - Strengths: Self-serve tools
   - Weaknesses: Not local-focused

### Service Delivery Feasibility

#### 1. Google Business Profile Optimization
**Feasibility: ✅ HIGH**
- Manual process: Create/claim listing, add photos, optimize description
- Tools needed: Google Business Profile API (free), photo editing
- Time per client: 2-4 hours initial, 30 min/month maintenance
- Deliverables: Verified listing, optimized profile, photo gallery

#### 2. Lead Capture Systems
**Feasibility: ✅ HIGH**
- Tools: Webflow/Framer for landing pages, Loops/Resend for email
- Database: Simple SQLite or Airtable
- Notifications: Telegram bot or email
- Time per client: 4-8 hours initial setup

#### 3. Google Ads Management
**Feasibility: ⚠️ MEDIUM (requires ad spend budget)**
- Tools: Google Ads API, Meta Ads Manager
- Client needs: Google Ads account + credit card
- Our role: Setup, optimize, report
- Challenge: Need minimum €200/mo ad spend to see results

#### 4. Full Growth Partnership
**Feasibility: ✅ HIGH (if we limit scope)**
- Combination of above services
- Dedicated Slack/chat channel
- Weekly calls
- Monthly retainer model
