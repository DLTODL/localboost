# LocalBoost Product Strategy

## Market Research: What Do Dutch Local Businesses ACTUALLY Need?

Based on analysis of local business pain points:

### Core Problems:
1. **Getting Found** - Not visible on Google/Maps
2. **Getting Reviews** - Hard to get customers to leave reviews
3. **Getting Leads** - Website doesn't convert
4. **Getting Time** - No time for marketing
5. **Getting Customers** - Hard to stand out from competitors

### What We Should Build (Product Ideas)

#### Tier 1: Quick Wins (Fast to build, immediate value)
1. **🔍 Lead Finder** - Find businesses that might need your services
   - Input: City + Industry
   - Output: List of potential customers with contact info
   - Value: Direct revenue generation
   
2. **⭐ Review Generator** - AI generates personalized review requests
   - Input: Business name, customer name
   - Output: SMS/WhatsApp/Email message + Google review link
   - Value: More reviews = more trust = more customers

3. **📱 Social Post Generator** - AI creates social media posts
   - Input: Business type + what to promote
   - Output: Post text + hashtags + optimal posting time
   - Value: Consistent social presence without effort

#### Tier 2: Differentiation (Medium effort, high unique value)
4. **📊 Local Keyword Finder** - What are locals searching for
   - Input: City + Industry
   - Output: Keywords + search volume + content ideas
   - Value: Know what to write about

5. **🏃 Competitor Scanner** - Spy on local competitors
   - Input: Competitor URL
   - Output: Their SEO score, keywords, ads, weaknesses
   - Value: Know what you're up against

6. **📋 Quote Generator** - Professional quotes in 2 clicks
   - Input: Services + prices
   - Output: Branded PDF quote
   - Value: Look professional, close more deals

#### Tier 3: Moonshots (Higher effort, recurring revenue potential)
7. **🤖 AI Receptionist** - WhatsApp chatbot for bookings
8. **📅 Appointment Scheduler** - Simple booking system
9. **💼 Mini CRM** - Track leads + follow-ups

## Design Principles

1. **Dark mode by default** - Modern, premium feel
2. **Instant results** - No loading screens, show value immediately
3. **Copy-to-clipboard** - Easy to use
4. **Single purpose** - One tool, one job, done well
5. **Mobile-first** - Local businesses often use phones
